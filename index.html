FAQ

Helpful answers for travelers

How do I book a massage in Da Nang as a foreign tourist?

The easiest option is messaging. Use the Book Now button, choose WhatsApp, Telegram, or Messenger, and send your preferred date, time, location, and treatment.

Which area is best for a spa visit in Da Nang?

My Khe Beach and An Thuong are convenient for resort and beach stays, while the city center is better for evening bookings near restaurants and the Han River.

Should I book the same day or in advance?

Same-day booking is often possible, but evenings and weekends fill faster. Booking a few hours ahead gives travelers more choice.

Are prices usually fixed?

Reputable wellness venues usually show fixed treatment prices. Review the published menu or confirm the full price in your booking chat before arrival.

What should I wear or bring?

Most spas provide everything needed for the treatment. Bringing your hotel name and preferred time is usually more important than bringing anything physical.
