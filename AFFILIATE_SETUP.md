# AFFILIATE SETUP

Guide for operating the site as an affiliate channel for legitimate massage and spa partners in Da Nang.

## How To Work With Local Spas

Start with a short, practical shortlist of partner venues instead of trying to cover every spa in the city.

Recommended partner selection criteria:

- clean public reputation
- transparent pricing
- tourist-friendly communication
- consistent availability
- willingness to respond on messaging platforms
- clear agreement on commissions or lead handling

Initial outreach checklist:

1. identify 10 to 15 candidate spas
2. review Google Maps and TripAdvisor signals
3. contact the owner or manager directly
4. explain that the site targets foreign tourists
5. confirm they can handle WhatsApp or Telegram leads quickly
6. agree on service categories, price ranges, and booking process

## How To Track Referrals

At minimum, every lead should capture:

- date
- source page
- source channel
- partner spa
- guest request type
- booking outcome
- commission status

Tracking options:

- Google Sheets
- Airtable
- Notion database
- CRM if lead volume grows

Use simple lead IDs such as:

```text
DNG-20260529-001
```

## WhatsApp Workflow

### Recommended flow

1. Visitor clicks `Book via WhatsApp`.
2. Visitor joins chat or group.
3. Operator asks:
   - preferred treatment
   - date
   - time
   - hotel area
   - number of guests
4. Operator routes the guest to the best-fit partner spa.
5. Operator records the lead outcome.

### Best practices

- reply fast
- use saved quick replies
- confirm total price before arrival
- confirm final spa, time, and treatment in one message
- avoid overpromising availability

## Telegram Workflow

### Recommended flow

1. Visitor clicks `Join Telegram Booking Group`.
2. Visitor enters the public booking group or direct Telegram contact flow.
3. Admin or operator responds with intake questions.
4. Partner spa is assigned based on area, availability, and treatment fit.
5. Final booking details are confirmed in chat.

### Best practices

- pin a welcome message in the booking group
- list supported treatments clearly
- specify local timezone
- clarify whether the group is for inquiry only or direct booking
- remove stale promotions quickly

## Suggested Commission Models

Use simple models first. Complexity can be added later if lead volume justifies it.

### Model 1: Fixed fee per completed booking

Example:

- `100,000 VND` to `300,000 VND` per completed customer

Best when:

- service prices vary
- the spa wants simple accounting

### Model 2: Percentage of treatment value

Example:

- `10%` to `20%` of completed treatment spend

Best when:

- higher-end packages are common
- partner trusts the reporting process

### Model 3: Flat monthly listing fee plus smaller booking bonus

Example:

- monthly placement fee
- plus a smaller fee per converted guest

Best when:

- partner wants guaranteed visibility
- you want more stable cash flow

### Model 4: Premium placement package

Example inclusions:

- homepage placement
- review page mention
- preferred lead routing
- special seasonal campaign slot

Best when:

- a spa wants more exposure than standard partners

## Partner Operations Checklist

- update partner rates monthly
- verify operating hours weekly
- check Google review trends regularly
- confirm response times on WhatsApp and Telegram
- remove partners who stop replying reliably
- keep affiliate terms documented in writing

## Legal And Trust Notes

- present the site as an independent guide
- disclose referral commissions clearly
- do not publish fake reviews
- do not imply ownership of partner spas unless true
- do not use adult or misleading wording

## Scaling Suggestions

Once conversions are stable:

- separate partners by area
- separate by budget tier
- add seasonal landing pages
- add booking source tags
- add lead tracking automation
- add a partner-facing status dashboard
