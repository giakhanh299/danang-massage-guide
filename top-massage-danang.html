FAQ

Quick answers before you book

What is the easiest area for first-time tourists?

An Thuong and My Khe Beach are often the easiest because they are close to many hotels and familiar tourist routes.

Should I trust the listed prices exactly?

Use them as a public reference point, but always confirm the latest menu and any promotions directly with the spa.

Can I book the same day?

Often yes, but evening slots are more competitive. Messaging a few hours in advance gives you better odds.

What should I do after choosing a spa?

Open Google Maps, confirm the exact branch, check the latest reviews, then send your booking request by WhatsApp or Telegram.
